Brandon Yoon

Film Enthusiast


My name is Brandon Yoon, and I will be graduating from UCR as a digital production major. As a film enthusiast, I’ve always been so fond of movies that I wanted to be a part of the filming process. I’m not much of a screenwriter but I loved editing videos. Editing might seem tedious but the work that goes into editing makes me feel peaceful. I’m also somewhat of a perfectionist that I love the detail-orientated culture of editing. Apart from editing, I want to learn visual effects and use it to tell stories. In addition, I also am a fan of sound because I believe we can see 80% of the things we hear in a movie. Audio affects the things we see as it draws focus to a particular thing on screen.With all of these ambitions, I ultimately would love to write and direct movies in the future for the public to see and react.
